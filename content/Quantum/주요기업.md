# Quantum Technology 주요 기업

## Big Tech / Platform

- IBM: superconducting quantum computer, Qiskit, quantum roadmap
- Google Quantum AI: superconducting qubit, quantum error correction
- Microsoft: Azure Quantum, quantum software ecosystem
- Amazon Braket: managed quantum cloud service
- Intel: silicon spin qubit and quantum control research

## Specialist / Hardware

- IonQ: trapped-ion quantum computer
- Rigetti: superconducting quantum processor
- Quantinuum: trapped-ion quantum computing and quantum software
- D-Wave: quantum annealing and hybrid quantum applications
- PsiQuantum: photonic quantum computing
